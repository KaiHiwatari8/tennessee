---
title: Clothing Checklist
description: List of things to pack when going to Smokey Mountains, Tennessee.
image:
dateUpdated: June 15, 2023
---

- Clothes and extra pairs for change
- Closed-toe comfortable hiking shoes, Crocs
- Swim suits, if you plan to swim in the waterfalls
- Sunglasses
- Hat or cap
- Rain jacket or poncho
