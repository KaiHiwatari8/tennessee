---
title: General Checklist
description: List of things to pack when going to Smokey Mountains, Tennessee.
image:
dateUpdated: June 15, 2023
---

- Lots of water, bottles if you can
- Sunscreen
- Bug spray
- Flashlight
- Football
- Energy or protein bars
- Pocket knife
- Driving gloves
- Headlamp
- Camera and its setup
- ID cards, especially if you are a student
